We’re Beyond Co., Ltd., an independent game studio based in Singapore. We are the creators of Beyond Hana, a visual novel that blends emotional storytelling with atmospheric art and music, creating an experience that lingers long after the game ends. Our team is small but deeply passionate about narrative-driven experiences. With a flat and collaborative structure, every voice in the team contributes to crafting worlds, characters, and choices that truly resonate with players. Right now, we’re working on our next visual novel — a story that explores “love and loss in a fragmented future”. At Beyond, we experiment and iterate to bring fresh, heartfelt experiences to players who love to lose themselves in meaningful stories.
